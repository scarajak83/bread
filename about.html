Our Story

Crafted with passion, served with love

Welcome to Bread N' Brew

At Bread N' Brew, we celebrate the art of baking and brewing in the heart of Berkeley Heights. Every croissant, pastry, and cup of coffee is crafted with passion and the finest ingredients sourced from trusted local and artisan producers.

Founded by Nally Sadri, our café represents the perfect union of artisan baking traditions and exceptional coffee culture. Our team has trained with master bakers and coffee roasters, bringing authentic expertise to every item we create.
